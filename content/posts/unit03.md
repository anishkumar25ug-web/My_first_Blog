Reflection Blog on WiKi Failure

When I was introduced to the topic “Right Kind of Wrong”, it was a completely new domain for me to understand the critique of failures and understand them in different perspectives. I had never thought that we can also study about failures as lessons. Before this assessment I used to see my own failure as negative and a sign of weakness. Now, I see my mistake as the process of learning and growth. I also got to know that the mistakes are obvious but shouldn't be ignored. They are to be taken seriously and corrected as soon as they are visible. In the case study which was assigned to me and my group was about the failure in the Digital market. In that case, it was a massive loss of around 460 million dollars in just 45 minutes. I could also remember the heavy loss of my time when I appeared twice in the APU entrance to qualify the University Exam. At that time when I didn't qualify, I started doubting myself and criticizing myself instead of correcting my mistakes. Later when I introspected then I started working on the mistake and also practiced a lot for the test. This could be a very small example of failure in my life that gave me the lesson to work on the tiny things to make big things better.The most good thing to take away from this assessment is to learn from mistakes and focus on the minute thing which can cause the bugs in my life or others too.
